Welcome to my junk drawer index! Here lies one one of many of my small collections, conglomerations of miscellanea. I am always considering the practice of collection, and the many forms in which it can manifest and be presented as a body both personally and through display. There is something to be said about every item in every collection that I have created for myself, and through this index I am discovering where to begin the process of thematic documentation and integration within my practice. I invite you to utilize this similarly to an i-spy, click and read and discover the layers of my junk drawer.
