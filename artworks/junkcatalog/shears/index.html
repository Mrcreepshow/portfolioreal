shears

I haven't gotten a professional haircut in probably two, maybe three years now. I hate to spend money on something that has too often left me in tears in the past. I gave myself bangs a little over a year ago now, the third attempt in a long, long succession of doing my own hair spanning all the way back to middle school. The first two attempts were miserable and I let them grow out immediately, but the third time is a charm and I have had them ever since the last try. These shears were purchased so that I could maintain them without having to use craft scissors.
