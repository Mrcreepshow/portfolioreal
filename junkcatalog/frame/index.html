tiny picture frame

I gathered this picture frame recently from a thrift store. I have begun a little collection of frames that I like, that I plan to hang somewhere someday, yet they all sit empty. It's a shame that physical photos are a little harder to acquire these days compared to my childhood and prior. My family has boxes on boxes of photos, yet I have seemingly none of my own. It feels like a much more roundabout process, formatting iphone photos and finding some access to a cheap printer. Someday soon I will have to collect all of the images I want and take a thumbdrive to a little kiosk in a grocery store, have them all printed. Until then I'm sure these frames will continue to accumulate, and continue to sit empty.
