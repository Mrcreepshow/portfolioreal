inhaler

I was prescribed this inhaler in 2021. I have allergies, but have never had asthma as far as I know. I was really sick, having lost my voice and struggling to breathe. I don't recall too much of it, but I remember trying not to pass out on the bus or on the street waiting for the bus (which seemed to take a fucking eternity to arrive) on my way home from gathering my prescriptions. The other medicine I had received was lidocaine viscous, and happened to be the most disgusting and foul thing I had ever encountered.
Anyways, I have held on to the inhaler ever since, just in case. It has really come in handy the last year or so, ever since I met my boyfriend who happens to have asthma. I'm glad that I kept it, that I know it will be here if he ever happens to forget his and there's an emergency. It has become an item of comfort.
