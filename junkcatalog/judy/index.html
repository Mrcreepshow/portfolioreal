a letter from my grandmother

This is a note I received from my grandmother on my mom's side. It was sent to accompany an envelope of jewelry from my great-grandmother following her passing this last year. The jewelry included two necklaces and a watch, all of which were very beautiful. (See also: Watches) This side of my family lives in South Dakota, quite a ways away from my family in Michigan, and due to other extrenuous circumstances we were unable to attend the funeral. My grandmother sent my mother, sisters and I images of the pieces that the rest of our relatives had gotten to select from so that we could remain involved despite being so far.
