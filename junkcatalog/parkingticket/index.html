unpaid parking ticket

One of many parking tickets that I have embarrassingly acquired since moving to my new apartment. Parking happens to be very sparse compared to my old place, with street cleaning more frequent. It is funny how easy it is for me to forget simple but important things, such as moving my car when I know the next day is a cleaning day. Comical. I have a decent method for sparing my bank account once I get a ticket, though. Once I inevitably garner a new violation, I will then pay the previous. In my head this is helpful, I dont let them pile up and I don't reeaaally have to pay it right away if I don't have the spare $60 lying around.
